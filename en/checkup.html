Insurance Portfolio Review

Full protection of your assets and quality of life — for you and your family

Proper risk management is the foundation of financial stability — protecting you and your family from unexpected events that could destabilise the household budget. A financial planner reviews all existing insurance coverage (health, life, disability, and long-term care) to ensure it fully and accurately meets your personal needs.

An essential part of the process is identifying duplications, which allows us to cancel unnecessary policies and significantly streamline costs. This optimisation creates direct monthly savings that can be redirected to investment channels or increased pension savings.
Ultimately, the goal is to achieve "peace of mind" knowing you are protected at minimum cost and maximum efficiency.

What does the insurance portfolio review process include?

Existing Coverage Scan
Comprehensive review of all policies: health, life, disability and long-term care
Eliminating Duplications
Identifying and cancelling or reducing unnecessary insurance policies without harming the required coverage
Direct Monthly Savings
Streamlining premium costs and freeing up budget for investment channels or pension savings
Precise Coverage for Your Needs
Aligning coverage to critical events: disability, death, severe illness
Ongoing Optimisation
Periodic update of coverage in line with life changes and economic circumstances
Peace of Mind
Full protection of your and your family's standard of living at minimum cost
