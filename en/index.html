About

Tal Gabay, Senior Financial Planner, Retirement Planner
M.B.A — Business Administration

My professional journey in the world of finance began at the heart of Israel's financial industry. In 2006, I served as a manager at Excellence Investment House, where I gained deep and unique insight into how capital markets operate "behind the scenes." The experience I accumulated in managing complex investment systems gave me the tools to analyze every financial opportunity in depth and act from a broad strategic perspective.

Driven by a desire to bring the high standards of the institutional capital market directly to our clients, we founded the firm in 2011. Today, we responsibly and professionally manage the financial future of hundreds of families across Israel.

Professional Guidance at Life's Key Crossroads

  • Financial & Pension Planning: Creating a comprehensive strategy for long-term wealth and savings management. We ensure your financial and pension assets are synchronized, management fees are optimized, and investment tracks are precisely aligned with your risk level and personal goals.
  • Smart Retirement Planning: Reaching retirement age is one of the most complex financial crossroads. We guide you through building a comprehensive retirement strategy — from maximizing tax benefits to optimizing pensions and savings, through to creating financial peace of mind for years ahead.
  • Risk Management & Insurance: Protecting what you have is the foundation of any financial plan. We review your family's insurance portfolio, eliminate duplications, update and reduce costs — so when the moment of truth arrives, you are truly protected.
  • Wealth Management & Intergenerational Transfer: A carefully orchestrated strategic process combining financial, legal and tax planning for the transfer of capital and assets — preserving their value over time and preventing disputes among heirs.

Why Choose Us?

The combination of broad academic education (M.B.A) and over 2 decades of field experience enables us to offer you:

  • Holistic Systemic View: Deep understanding of financial, pension and tax products, connecting them into one complete family picture.
  • Full Objectivity: Absolute commitment to your interests — not owned by any insurance company or investment house.
  • High-Standard Personal Service: Boutique-level portfolio management, where you are always at the center and receive professional, transparent and consistent service.
