Wills & Inheritance Planning

Smart intergenerational wealth transfer — bulletproof will and estate management 2026

Don't leave the future of your estate to chance. A professionally drafted will — adapted to the legal and technological reality of 2026 — is the surest way to ensure your wishes are honoured and your family is protected.

In a world where digital assets, cryptocurrency and online accounts form part of every estate, proper inheritance planning requires specialist expertise. We accompany you at every stage — from drafting the will to distributing the estate — combining legal mastery with full personal support.

Common concerns — and our solution

Concern:
Family disputes, a contested or forged will
✓ Solution 2026:
A "bulletproof" will prepared by a specialist lawyer with strong digital documentation
Concern:
Will changed by a second spouse, children's rights at risk
✓ Solution 2026:
Mutual will with a rigid "heir after heir" mechanism — children's rights are protected
Concern:
Loss of digital assets, crypto or online accounts
✓ Solution 2026:
Full estate plan including digital wallets, crypto holdings and online accounts
